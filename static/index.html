About Us

Zenith Perspective is a non profit organization dedicated towards educating and guiding K-12 students through the STEM and Business fields. We are a peer tutoring organization, conducting a plethora of workshops and tutoring sessions (see services). Our goal is to give back to the community the gift of knowledge.

We have a vast library of educational content to help students excel through high school academics and extracurricular activities and prepare for university.

We also conduct philanthropical activities. Some of our deeds include donating masks to healthcare workers and raising funds for a COVID relief programs.
